Roles and Responsibilities

Leadership

Provide strategic direction and ensure the alignment of organizational goals with the welfare of veterans.

Advocacy

Represent veterans' interests in policy discussions and ensure their voices are heard at all governmental levels.

Healthcare Support

Facilitate access to medical services and promote health initiatives tailored for veterans and their families.

Employment Assistance

Offer career counseling, job placement services, and skill development programs to aid veterans in securing meaningful employment.

Community Engagement

Organize events and programs that foster community involvement and strengthen the bond among veterans.

Educational Initiatives

Provide scholarships, training sessions, and workshops to support the continuous learning of veterans and their dependents.
