Our Legends

Indian Army

Field Marshal Sam Manekshaw

Born: April 3, 1914 | Died: June 27, 2008

Awards: Padma Vibhushan, Padma Bhushan

Led India to victory in the 1971 Indo-Pak war, instrumental in the creation of Bangladesh.

General K. S. Thimayya

Born: March 31, 1906 | Died: December 17, 1965

Awards: Padma Bhushan

Led India's military modernization and played a vital role in global peacekeeping.

Indian Navy

Admiral S. M. Nanda

Born: October 10, 1915 | Died: May 11, 2009

Awards: Padma Vibhushan

Strategized the naval blockade of Karachi during the 1971 war.

Admiral R. H. Tahiliani

Born: May 12, 1930 | Died: October 14, 2015

Awards: Param Vishisht Seva Medal

A key figure in naval aviation and submarine operations.

Indian Air Force

Marshal of the Air Force Arjan Singh

Born: April 15, 1919 | Died: September 16, 2017

Awards: Padma Vibhushan

The only IAF officer with a five-star rank, instrumental in the 1965 war.

Wing Commander K. K. Majumdar

Born: September 6, 1913 | Died: February 17, 1945

Awards: Distinguished Flying Cross

One of India's most decorated pilots of WWII.
