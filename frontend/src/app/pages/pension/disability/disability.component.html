Disability Pension for Veterans

Ensuring dignity and financial security for those who served with honor.

Who is Eligible?

🩺 Medically Boarded Out

Veterans discharged due to service-related injuries or disabilities.

⚖️ Minimum Service Criteria

No minimum service required if disability is due to military service.

🔹 Disability Percentage

Must have a disability rating of 20% or more, as per medical assessment.

📜 Pension Approval

Approved by the Armed Forces Medical Board (AFMB) and the government.

Disability Pension Benefits

💰 Monthly Pension

Tax-free pension based on disability percentage & rank.

🏥 Medical Benefits

Access to ECHS (Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme).

🎖️ Additional Allowances

Higher disability percentages receive extra financial benefits.

🏡 Family Pension

Ensures security for dependents in case of death due to disability.

How to Apply for Disability Pension?

Follow these steps to apply for disability pension benefits.

1️⃣ Gather Documents

Service records, disability medical reports, and ID proof.

2️⃣ Submit to SPARSH

Apply online via the SPARSH Portal.

3️⃣ Medical Board Review

AFMB assesses disability and issues approval.

4️⃣ Pension Disbursement

Approved pension is credited monthly to the veteran’s account.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum disability percentage required ❓

20% or more as assessed by the Armed Forces Medical Board.

Can I receive both service and disability pension ❓

Yes, both pensions can be availed if eligible.

How is disability pension calculated ❓

Based on disability percentage, last drawn salary, and rank.
