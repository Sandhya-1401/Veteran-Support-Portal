Family Pension for Dependents of Veterans

Honoring the sacrifices of our heroes by securing their families’ future.

Types of Family Pension

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Ordinary Family Pension

Given to the spouse or eligible dependents after the death of a veteran.

⚔️ Special Family Pension

For dependents of veterans who lost their lives due to service-related causes.

🎖️ Liberalized Family Pension

For dependents of those killed in action, equal to the last drawn salary.

Family Pension Benefits

💰 Monthly Pension

Financial support based on service & rank of the deceased veteran.

🏥 Medical Benefits

Access to ECHS (Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme) for dependents.

🎓 Educational Support

Scholarships for children under welfare schemes.

🏡 Additional Allowances

Housing & disability support for eligible dependents.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is eligible for the family pension ❓

The spouse, dependent children, or parents of a deceased veteran.

Can both the spouse and child receive a pension ❓

Generally, the spouse is the first beneficiary. If deceased, then the children.

What is the duration of the family pension ❓

For a spouse – lifelong. For children – up to 25 years or if disabled.
