Eligibility & Application Process

Follow these steps to check your eligibility and apply for pension benefits.

1️⃣ Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the service duration and other criteria for the pension type.

2️⃣ Gather Documents

Collect required documents such as discharge papers, service records, and ID proofs.

3️⃣ Submit Application

Apply through the official SPARSH Portal or visit the nearest Zila Sainik Board.

4️⃣ Pension Disbursement

Once verified, your pension will be disbursed through your bank account.

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Who is eligible for a pension?

All ex-servicemen who have completed the required tenure in service are eligible.

❓ How can I check my pension status?

You can check your pension status on the SPARSH Portal.

❓ Can I apply online?

Yes, applications can be submitted through the SPARSH Portal.
