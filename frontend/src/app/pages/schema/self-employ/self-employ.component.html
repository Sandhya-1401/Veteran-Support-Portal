Self Employment Schemes for Veterans

The Indian government offers multiple self-employment schemes to help veterans transition into business and entrepreneurship. Here are some key opportunities for ex-servicemen to establish a stable livelihood.

Loan Assistance for Ex-Servicemen

Get financial aid to start a business or retail outlet through government-backed loans.

DGR Training Programs

Specialized training programs by the Directorate General Resettlement to enhance business skills.

Explore Training

MSME Business Support

Assistance for veterans to set up and expand Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises.

Get Details

E-Commerce & Franchise Support

Guidance for ex-servicemen to establish e-commerce stores or franchise businesses.

Read More

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be an ex-serviceman or war widow.
  • Should possess basic business knowledge or willingness to undergo training.
  • Must meet financial eligibility requirements set by the government.

How to Apply?

Veterans can apply through official portals like DGR India or visit nearby Veteran Welfare Offices.
