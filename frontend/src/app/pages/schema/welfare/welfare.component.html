Welfare Schemes for Veterans & Their Families

The Government of India provides several welfare schemes to support the well-being of veterans, war widows, and their families. These initiatives aim to ensure financial stability, medical assistance, and educational support.

Ex-Servicemen Health Scheme (ECHS)

Provides cashless medical treatment to veterans and their dependents through empaneled hospitals.

Housing & Land Allocation

Subsidized housing schemes for ex-servicemen, including priority allotment.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must be a retired defense personnel or war widow.
  • Dependents of veterans are eligible for educational schemes.
  • Financial aid is subject to government policy guidelines.

How to Apply?

Eligible veterans and families can apply through official portals like DGR India or visit their nearest Veteran Welfare Office.
